How to make a PB & J sandwich

  1. Aquire a knife, peanut butter, jelly, and two slices of bread.
  2. Slather peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Slather jelly on the other slice.
  4. Put the slices together by the slathered sides.
  5. Enjoy!
